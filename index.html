Mapping Malaysia’s Culinary Landscape

This map shows 281 food businesses across Malaysia. Kuala Lumpur,

Selangor, and Penang lead with dense clusters. Urban growth and

tourism drive these thriving food hubs. Johor and Melaka showcase rich

heritage and cross-border flavours. East Malaysia’s Sabah and Sarawak

show emerging growth. Each dot reflects local passion and culinary

entrepreneurship. From Penang’s hawker stalls to Sabah’s seaside cafes,

these regions celebrate Malaysia’s diverse food and culture.

Geography as well as economy shape where flavours come alive.

Together, they form Malaysia’s vibrant, ever-evolving foodscape.
