From Plates to Patterns

Insights from Food Reviews and Business Data across Malaysia

Exploring reviews, business growth, and regional F&B trends through data visualisation

What the Map Shows

This map plots 281 F&B establishments across Malaysia. Use the legend to highlight a region and compare its footprint to the rest.

  • Urban hubs show denser clusters.
  • East Malaysia has fewer but growing nodes along key corridors.
  • Hover points for the business name and locality.

How to read it

Each dot represents an establishment. Grid lines help approximate latitude/longitude, while the outline is fixed—no reflow—so spatial comparisons remain consistent.
